ABOUT US

The Sangguniang Kabataan (SK) of Barangay Lepaga is dedicated to empowering the youth and fostering a vibrant, engaged community. We strive to create opportunities, promote active participation, and champion the interests of young people in our barangay.

OUR MISSION

To serve as the voice of the youth, to lead with integrity, and to implement programs that drive positive change and sustainable development in Barangay Lepaga.

OUR VISION

A united and progressive youth sector that is actively involved in nation-building, equipped with the skills and values to be future leaders.

VALUES

Integrity, Service, Innovation, and Unity.
